傅立叶转换
----------------------
.. math::
   :name: Fourier transform

   (\mathcal{F}f)(y)
    = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi}^{\ n}}
      \int_{\mathbb{R}^n} f(x)\,
      e^{-\mathrm{i} y \cdot x} \,\mathrm{d} x.
